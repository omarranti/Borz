What is PPF Wrapping?

Paint Protection Film (PPF) is a transparent, self-healing polyurethane film that acts as an invisible shield for your vehicle's paint. Our precision installation process ensures seamless coverage that's virtually undetectable while providing maximum protection.

Dubai-Specific Benefits

  • • Desert Sand Protection: Shields against fine sand abrasion from Dubai's frequent sandstorms
  • • Heat Resistance: Withstands Dubai's extreme temperatures (up to 50°C) without yellowing or peeling
  • • Stone Chip Defense: Protects against highway debris and stone chips common on Dubai roads
  • • UV Protection: Prevents paint oxidation and fading from intense sun exposure
  • • Self-Healing: Minor scratches and swirls disappear with heat exposure
