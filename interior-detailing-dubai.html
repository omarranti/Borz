What is Interior Detailing?

Interior detailing is a comprehensive deep-cleaning and restoration process for your vehicle's cabin. Our professional service removes embedded sand, dust, and debris while protecting and restoring leather, fabric, and surfaces to showroom condition.

Dubai-Specific Benefits

  • • Sand & Dust Removal: Eliminates embedded desert sand and fine dust particles from Dubai's frequent sandstorms
  • • Leather Protection: Prevents leather cracking and fading from Dubai's extreme heat (50°C+) and intense UV exposure
  • • Dashboard UV Protection: Shields dashboards and interior surfaces from sun damage and warping
  • • Sanitization: Eliminates bacteria and odors that thrive in Dubai's high humidity environment
  • • Odor Removal: Removes musty smells and food odors common in hot, humid climates

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about interior detailing

How much does interior detailing cost in Dubai?

Interior detailing prices vary based on vehicle size, condition, and specific services needed. Basic packages start from AED 500, while comprehensive restoration services can range from AED 1,500-3,000. Contact us for a personalized quote based on your vehicle.

How long does interior detailing take?

Standard interior detailing typically takes 3-5 hours. Deep cleaning and restoration services may require 6-8 hours. We'll provide an accurate timeline during your initial consultation.

What's included in interior detailing?

Our interior detailing includes deep vacuuming, steam cleaning, leather conditioning, dashboard protection, window cleaning, sanitization, and odor removal. Specific services depend on your chosen package.

Can you restore leather seats?

Yes, we specialize in leather restoration. Our services include cleaning, conditioning, crack repair, color restoration, and UV protection to prevent future damage from Dubai's harsh climate.

How often should I get interior detailing?

For Dubai's climate, we recommend interior detailing every 3-4 months. Vehicles exposed to frequent sandstorms or heavy use may benefit from more frequent service. Regular maintenance helps protect your investment.

Do you clean car carpets and mats?

Absolutely. We provide deep cleaning for carpets, floor mats, and trunk liners. This includes vacuuming, steam cleaning, stain removal, and deodorizing to remove embedded sand and dust.

What products do you use for interior detailing?

We use premium, pH-balanced products specifically designed for automotive interiors. All products are safe for leather, fabric, and sensitive surfaces, and are formulated to withstand Dubai's extreme temperatures.
