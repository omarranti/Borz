FAQ

Do you offer warranty on PPF & coating? +

Yes — warranty options are available depending on the package and materials selected. Message us to confirm coverage.

How long does a full detail take? +

Timing depends on vehicle size and condition. We'll give an accurate estimate when you send photos on WhatsApp or Telegram.

Can I wait while the work is done? +

Yes — there's a lounge area upstairs with tea and coffee while you wait.

Do you support English / Arabic / Russian? +

Yes — our team can support English, Arabic, and Russian speaking clients.

What's the best detailing for supercars in Dubai? +

For supercars in Dubai's harsh climate, we recommend our Premium Protection Package: full PPF wrap + ceramic coating. This combination provides maximum protection against desert sand, intense UV rays, and heat (up to 50°C), keeping your supercar's paint pristine year-round.

How much does PPF installation cost in Dubai? +

PPF pricing varies by vehicle size, coverage area (full wrap vs. partial), and film quality. Contact us via WhatsApp or Telegram with your vehicle details and photos for a personalized quote. We offer competitive pricing with warranty coverage.

Does ceramic coating protect against Dubai's heat? +

Yes — our nano-ceramic coatings are specifically formulated for extreme heat conditions. They provide superior UV protection (up to 800°C heat resistance), prevent paint oxidation, and create a hydrophobic layer that repels dust and sand, making Dubai's frequent sandstorms easier to clean.

Do you work on luxury brands like Lamborghini, Ferrari, McLaren? +

Absolutely — we specialize in luxury and supercar detailing. Our team has extensive experience with Lamborghini, Ferrari, McLaren, Porsche, Bentley, Rolls-Royce, and other premium brands. We use premium materials and techniques specifically designed for high-end vehicles.

How do I book an appointment? +

Booking is easy — contact us via WhatsApp (+971 56 329 9615) or Telegram for instant response. Send photos of your vehicle and we'll provide a quote and schedule your appointment. We're open daily from 9 AM to 8 PM in Al Quoz, Dubai.
